What's StudentDevs?

We are a Community of students in Surebloom who are interested in technology. StudentDevs started during the 2022/2023 Academic Session. Our blog is StudentDevs, so feel free to visit it to read interesting facts on different technological trends (just don't forget to credit us for the articles!)

Within our team, we have students interested in UI/UX designs, Product Management, Front-end and Backend development, Data Science and Technical Writing .
